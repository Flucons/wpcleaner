The files in the images directory and its subdirectories are from :
- Tango:
  The Tango Desktop Project (http://tango.freedesktop.org/).
  Icons from Tango are in the public domain.
  Image names have been prefixed by tango-.
- Gnome:
  The Gnome Icon Theme (http://git.gnome.org./browse/gnome-icon-theme/tree/ - http://www.gnome.org/).
  Icons from Gnome are under GPL v2.
  Image names have been prefixed by gnome-.
- Commons:
  Wikimedia Commons (http://commons.wikimedia.org).
  Licence depends on the image.
  Image names have been prefixed by commons-.
